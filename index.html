CSUN Chapter

Association for Computing Machinery at CSUN

ACM at CSUN is a student-led community for curious builders. We host hands-on workshops, industry talks, and peer mentorship to help members sharpen their skills and build standout portfolios. Whether you're just starting out or preparing for internships, you'll find teammates, guidance, and real opportunities to create.
