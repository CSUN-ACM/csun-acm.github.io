About Us

ACM @ CSUN is a dynamic student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery, dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and career readiness in the tech space.

Through hands-on workshops, engaging speaker events, coding challenges, and social meetups, we build a supportive community where students of all skill levels can grow together. Whether you're into AI, software development, cybersecurity, or just starting out, there's a place for you here.
