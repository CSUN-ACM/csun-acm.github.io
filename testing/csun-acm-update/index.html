At CSUN, we believe in inclusivity, hard work, and determination and here at ACM we cherish and promote those values. Come join us at our next meeting and see the amazing guest speakers, workshops, and social events we can offer you. We promise that you'll never be bored and that you'll always be challenged by our new and innovative event We understand that many students have very intense course loads. We're here for you when you need to kick back and enjoy the company of some amazing people. Come and talk to us today! Currently our events are posted in our Discord.
