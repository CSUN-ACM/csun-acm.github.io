Membership & Joining

How can I join the club/chapter?

To join our organization, CSUN Students can join through the CSUN MataSync Portal CSUN MataSync Portal. Please log in using your CSUN student account in MataSync, after you log in, select the option to request to join. After joining through MataSync, you will receive an email from our parent organization, the Association of Computing Machinery (ACM), which will contain directions on how to log in to their Association of Computing Machinery website.
If you are not able to log in, please use the option 'Trouble Logging In?' on the site and use your CSUN student email to access your account.
This is the link to access your ACM account: https://myacm.acm.org/

Is there a membership fee?

There are no fees or dues. CSUN Students that join our organization through CSUN's MataSync get a free student membership that lasts for a year.

What are the benefits of becoming a member?

As a member, you will have access to online subscriptions, Communications of the ACM and XRDS: Crossroads ACM's Student Magazine through the ACM organization website. These are among some benefits and resources that the ACM organization provides. Our CSUN ACM Chapter will be setting up monthly events here at CSUN, including workshops, seminars, and other coding related events for all CSUN students. All members will be informed of these upcoming events through their student email and through our discord channel. We will also post any upcoming events on our CSUN ACM website's event section.
