Frequently Asked Questions

  How can I join the club/chapter?

CSUN students can join our organization through the CSUN MataSync Portal. Log in with your CSUN student account, request to join, and you will receive an email from the Association of Computing Machinery (ACM) with login instructions for their site.
If you have trouble logging in, use the 'Trouble Logging In?' option on the site and access your account with your CSUN student email.
Click below to login to your ACM Account

Is there a membership fee?

There are no fees or dues. CSUN Students that join our organization through CSUN's MataSync get a free student membership that lasts for a year.

What are the benefits of becoming a member

As a member, you'll enjoy online subscriptions to Communications of the ACM and XRDS: Crossroads ACM's Student Magazine. The CSUN ACM Chapter hosts monthly events like workshops, seminars, and coding sessions. Stay informed about events via student email, our Discord channel, and our CSUN ACM website.

How often does the club/chapter meet?

Our CSUN ACM Chapter organizes monthly workshops, seminars, and coding events for all CSUN students. Stay informed about upcoming on-campus and virtual (Zoom) events through your student email and our Discord channel.
